Welcome to the the game that conquered the world!

PaCovid! The virus that changed the world!
If you want to win in the game, you have to help PaCovid conquer all the countries before all the vaccines catch him! So how do you do that?! Eat all countries and can earn at least 100 points before the time runs out.
Good Luck!
